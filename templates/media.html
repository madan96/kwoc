The countdown's over. Time for the big release of #KWOC!

As the semester is about to end, we are here with something you can make good use of, in your December vacations, we call it Kharagpur Winter of Code (KWoC). It is a December long online program. For those who already know about Google Summer of Code, KWoC is going to be very helpful for you. Register yourself as student and the instructions will follow you. For those who don't know about such open source programs, KWoC is a platform where you are going to learn about a new style of collaborative software development.

So, take some time and visit

http://kwoc.kossiitkgp.in/

and register yourself.

Happy endsems!

- KOSS
